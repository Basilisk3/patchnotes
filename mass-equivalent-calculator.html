Type the mass cost of your units and press Calculate. For example Percy (1280 mass) vs Harbinger (840 mass).

vs

The grey fields are for setting fixed costs. For example if you want to count the cost of a T3 HQ for testing Harbringers vs Pillars, you enter the cost of the HQ under the Harbringers. Or if you want to test UEF battleships vs Cybran battleships, and add 5 shields to your UEF battleships, you write 5*1300 under the UEF battleships.
