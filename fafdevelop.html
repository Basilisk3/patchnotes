Bug fixes

Construction drones

GitHub references: 5040 and 5028

The construction drones of the UEF faction are prime candidates for various tasks. Due to a bug they could be ignored as a target as anti air weapons scan for targets in their vicinity. As a result the drones are now a lot more vulnerable to anti air.

  • Rover drones (from the kennel) are no longer ignored as a target after docking once
  • Shoulder drones (of the UEF ACU) are now ignored as a target by weapons when they are docked on the shoulders

Missile construction

GitHub references: 5034, 4867 and 15

There have been long standing problems with missile construction that have been confusing players for years. With thanks to an assembly patch we can tackle two of these problems.

  • An adjacency bonus to structures building a missile is processed immediately
  • The progress of the missile construction is restored when a unit changes army

Graphics

Water ramp

GitHub references: 4964 and 5005

The water ramp is a texture that helps a map author tweak the appearance of (deep) water. Up to now the water ramp was only applied to the terrain. Units would receive only the water surface color. We now have an approach to apply the water ramp to units, props and shields too. This improves the overall immersion where units now truly feel like they are underwater.





Water sun reflections

GitHub references: 4931

You can't reflect something that is occluded. Previously the water would still reflect the sun, regardless whether it was occluded. The change is subtle, but it adds a lot to the final image once you see it.



Water unit reflections

GitHub references: 4696

A common effect for water is that it refracts what is underwater. There was a bug in the shader code that would also reflect bits of units that are above water, creating an odd orange haze on the water surface.
